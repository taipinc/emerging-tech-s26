# Reading 2: Datasets

Christo Buschek & Jer Thorp, *Models All The Way Down*

Read at: [https://knowingmachines.org/models-all-the-way](https://knowingmachines.org/models-all-the-way)

---

## Prompts for reading responses:

1.

The "aesthetic" subset used to fine-tune Midjourney was rated largely by a handful of users described as Western, Educated, Industrialized, Rich, and Democratic — whose "aesthetic preferences dominate the dataset". 

Whose taste is your image generator trained on? Does that change how you think about using it?

---
2.

The piece describes a circularity: datasets trained on models trained on datasets. What happens to errors, biases, or blind spots in that loop? 

Can you think of other systems (social, institutional, photographic) where this kind of circularity produces distortions?
