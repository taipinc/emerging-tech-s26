# Project 1: Interactive Camera
Due: week 9 / April 6

For this project, you will program a simple interactive camera application using p5.js (or other technologies as required). 

Using an algorithmic approach (with or without Machine Learning), **your camera will do something new, that no regular camera can do!**

Your camera app will receive a photographic input:
- A video feed from your webcam
- A still image from your webcam
- An uploaded image or video from your computer (pre-captured in the field)

Your unique camera algorithm will process the input and produce a new output, which can be:
- A modified video
- A modified still image
- An interactive application that others can use


## Project Proposal 
Due: week 7 / March 23

For your project proposal, think about what you want to make. There might be limitations to what **can** be achieved, but for the propsal, you can dream big!
